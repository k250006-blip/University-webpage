Frequently Asked Questions

When is the application deadline?

Fall applications are due by March 31st. Spring applications are due by October 15th.

What is the average class size?

Our average class size is 25 students for upper-level courses and 35 for introductory courses.

Do you offer online programs?

Yes, we offer fully online and hybrid programs in many disciplines.

What financial aid options are available?

We offer scholarships, grants, loans, and work-study opportunities.
